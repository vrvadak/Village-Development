Rooted in community

We connect smallholders and craftspeople with neighbors near and far. Every purchase supports rural livelihoods and preserves traditional skills.

Founded in 2023 by a group of passionate individuals from Campus Hub, Village Market emerged from a desire to create sustainable economic opportunities for rural communities. We believe in the power of technology to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern consumer needs.

Our platform showcases the finest organic produce, handmade crafts, and wholesome staples directly from local farmers and artisans. By eliminating middlemen, we ensure fair prices for producers while offering quality products to consumers.

Roads & Drainage

üõ£Ô∏è

Project Overview

Our roads and drainage project aims to transform village connectivity and prevent seasonal flooding that damages crops and homes.

Key Components

  • Construction of 15 km of all-weather roads connecting all households
  • Installation of 50 culverts for proper water flow
  • Drainage systems along main roads to prevent waterlogging
  • Bridge construction over seasonal streams
  • Road maintenance program with community participation

Benefits

  • Year-round access to markets, schools, and healthcare
  • Reduced crop loss due to flooding
  • Improved emergency response times
  • Enhanced property values
  • Better access for agricultural equipment

Progress

65% completed - 10 km of roads finished

Electricity & Lighting

üîÜ

Project Overview

Bringing reliable electricity to every household through a combination of solar power and grid connectivity.

Key Components

  • Installation of 200 solar streetlights throughout the village
  • Solar panels for 150 households with battery backup
  • Connection to the main power grid for commercial areas
  • Community charging stations for mobile devices
  • Energy-efficient LED bulbs distribution

Benefits

  • Safer travel after sunset for women and children
  • Extended productive hours for small businesses
  • Better educational opportunities with evening study
  • Improved healthcare services with refrigeration
  • Reduced kerosene usage and indoor air pollution

Progress

40% completed - 80 streetlights installed

Water Supply

üíß

Project Overview

Ensuring clean, reliable water access for all families through multiple sustainable sources.

Key Components

  • Construction of 5 deep borewells with submersible pumps
  • Installation of 50 rainwater harvesting systems
  • Water purification units at community collection points
  • Pipeline network connecting all households
  • Water storage tanks with distribution system

Benefits

  • Reduced time spent collecting water (especially for women)
  • Improved health outcomes with clean drinking water
  • Better crop irrigation possibilities
  • Reduced waterborne diseases
  • Increased agricultural productivity

Progress

75% completed - 4 borewells operational

Sanitation

üöÆ

Project Overview

Creating a cleaner, healthier village environment through improved sanitation facilities and waste management.

Key Components

  • Construction of 200 household toilets with septic tanks
  • 5 public toilet facilities in community areas
  • Waste segregation system with color-coded bins
  • Community composting pits for organic waste
  • Regular waste collection and transportation service

Benefits

  • Improved public health and reduced disease transmission
  • Cleaner village environment
  • Production of organic fertilizer for farming
  • Reduced open defecation
  • Enhanced dignity and safety for women

Progress

55% completed - 110 toilets constructed
